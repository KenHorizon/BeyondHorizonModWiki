Blazing Inferno

The Blazing Inferno is boss that can be fought in Old Ruined Fortress

Defeating them will provide the player with Wildfire Fragments, a crafting material for various equipment.

STATISTIC

Type: Boss

Environment: Nether

Health: 700

Defense: 15 || Damage Reduction: 15%

Knockback Resist: 100%

Follow Range: 70

Damage Cap: 40

Immune: Explosion, Fire, Lava, Arrows (If Shield is active)

BEHAVIOR

Blazing Inferno behave like a normal blaze but not hover when shooting fireballs. All Blazing Inferno's ability

take 5 seconds charge up to release/fire it

Blazing Inferno will take only 50% (-25% per missing shield) Damage dealt if the all shield are active

Blazing Inferno will turn enrage if the player is outside the boss arena, Increasing its movement speed and damage
